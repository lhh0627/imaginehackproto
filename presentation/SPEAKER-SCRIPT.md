# Cloud Sentinel — 5-Minute Speaker Script

**Total target: ~5:00** · **10 slides** · Pace: calm and confident, not rushed.

---

## Slide 1 — Title (~20s)

> "Good [morning/afternoon]. We're presenting **Secure & Energy-Aware Cloud Sentinel** — an automated operations dashboard built for Hilti's construction-tech cloud. One product that handles **security and sustainability** together."

---

## Slide 2 — The Problem (~35s)

> "On every jobsite, teams rely on cloud workloads — BIM rendering, telemetry, project data. But these systems are often **exposed to the internet** and **wasting energy** at the same time. Security tools don't show carbon impact. Sustainability tools don't catch open ports. And when something goes wrong, teams rarely know **who did what** or **how to fix it fast**."

---

## Slide 3 — Why Hilti (~35s)

> "Hilti stands for **reliability on the jobsite**. That promise should extend to the digital layer. Cloud Sentinel protects **project data**, reduces **idle cloud waste and carbon**, cuts **unnecessary cost**, and gives **continuous compliance** — not a once-a-year audit. It's the guardrail that keeps digital infrastructure as trustworthy as Hilti's physical tools."

---

## Slide 4 — Meet Cloud Sentinel (~25s)

> "Cloud Sentinel is that guardrail. It **scans continuously**, **diagnoses root causes**, shows **carbon and cost at risk**, and lets operators send **one-click alerts** to workload owners. Think of it as a Hilti tool for your cloud — always on, always checking."

---

## Slide 5 — How It Works (~30s)

> "Here's the flow. The sentinel agent scans workloads and cloud firewall rules every few seconds. When it finds a problem — say a BIM render server exposed to the internet — it traces the full route from internet to workload, surfaces it on the dashboard, and lets the operator alert the responsible team. Scan, correlate, diagnose, alert."

---

## Slide 6 — Security (~40s)

> "On security: we don't just say 'you have a vulnerability.' We show the **full exposure path** — internet, firewall rule, public endpoint, host port. Our **Exposure Diagnosis** explains who opened the port, the change ticket, why it was opened, whether it should stay open, the consequences, and the recommended fix. That's actionable intelligence, not another red badge."

---

## Slide 7 — Sustainability (~35s)

> "For sustainability, every workload shows energy, carbon, cost, owner, and project. We compare live usage against expected baselines. If a service is **idle but still consuming power**, we flag it as **zombie suspected**. If it's active but excessive, it's **over baseline**. Often, the same forgotten server is both a **security risk and a carbon leak** — Cloud Sentinel catches both."

---

## Slide 8 — Remediation (~35s)

> "Remediation is deliberate, not reckless. The operator clicks **Send worker alert**. The BIM worker's screen shows a visible alert. The owner acknowledges and closes it. The dashboard updates the incident timeline and risk posture. Real operations need **human-in-the-loop** — we alert the right person and create an audit trail."

---

## Slide 9 — Business Impact (~30s)

> "For Hilti, the impact is clear: **lower cloud spend**, **lower carbon**, **stronger security**, and **digital trust** that matches the brand. Operations teams get one daily control plane — scan volume, compliance status, incidents, and scheduled reporting. One idle BIM node can cost hundreds per month; Cloud Sentinel finds it in the first scan cycle."

---

## Slide 10 — Demo & Close (~45s)

> "Let me show you." *(Switch to live demo or screenshots.)*

**Demo order (if live):**
1. Open dashboard — point to operations KPIs
2. Show `BIM-Render-04` workload card — security + sustainability metrics
3. Open **Exposure Diagnosis** — root cause, ticket, recommended fix
4. Point to baseline status (normal / over baseline / zombie)
5. Click **Send worker alert**
6. Show worker page — red banner → **Acknowledge and close**
7. Return to dashboard — incident logged

> "That's **Cloud Sentinel** — security and sustainability operations for construction cloud. Because Hilti's digital infrastructure should be as dependable as everything on the jobsite. Thank you — happy to take questions."

## Tips for delivery

- **Slide 6 or 7** is your strongest "judge wow" moment — pick based on audience (security vs sustainability).
- **Slide 10 demo** is mandatory if you have a running environment; use screenshots if not.
- Don't read bullet points — tell stories ("one BIM server, port 8081, open to the world").
- End every slide with one sentence that links back to **Hilti reliability**.
